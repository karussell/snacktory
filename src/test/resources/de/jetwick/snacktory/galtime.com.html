Kris Humphries Avoids Kim Talk On GMA
By Andrea Krivelow, GalTime Entertainment Diva
on Friday, December 9, 2011
Comments | Blog about this
print

It was his first TV interview since the end of his 72-day marriage, and Kris Humphries took all steps possible to avoid answering questions about his union with Kim Kardashian.

He spoke about moving forward and focusing on what he loves:

"Through everything, I'm just focused on family and preparing for basketball. For me, it's just, certain things happen in life and you've got to move forward. I'm excited to be where I'm at right now, with the NBA season coming up."

When asked about his request to have the marriage annulled, Kris answered:

"Really, right now, I'm focused on basketball, and other things will take care of themselves.".

As for having his love life played out on camera, the NBA player is not one to watch the new season of Kourtney and Kim Take New York:

"The only things I really watch on TV of myself is playing basketball. I've never been someone to really watch the show on TV or anything like that. I don't really play into all the gossip."

Oh my gosh, how uncomfortable does he look?! Honestly - what did he think he was getting himself into when he agreed to be interviewed on Good Morning America?
